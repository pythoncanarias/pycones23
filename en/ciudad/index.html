"La Laguna"

San Cristóbal de La Laguna, better known as La Laguna, is the second most populous city in Tenerife and the third in the Canary Islands. It is located in the northeast of the island of Tenerife, next to the capital Santa Cruz de Tenerife, and is part of the metropolitan area.

The city was declared a World Heritage Site by Unesco in 1999 for being a unique example of an unwalled colonial city. Its name comes from the fact that it was built on an old lagoon that existed in the area.

Historically, the Canary Islands have been a strategic point -cultural, social and economic- between Europe and Latin America.

Transportation by Guagua (Bus) in Tenerife

To move comfortably around Tenerife, the transport system in "guagua" (bus) next to the tram, are the most convenient and cheapest options. Network of buses on the island cover a wide area, connecting the main cities, towns and tourist attractions. Here are some things that should to know about bus transportation in Tenerife:

  • Routes and Schedules: The bus service in Tenerife is extensive and has numerous routes that cover the entire island. They can get information about the routes and schedules at bus stops, as well as on the official website from the transport company Titsa. Be sure to check the hours of the buses, since they can vary depending on the day of the week.

  • Fares and Tickets: There is also the option of purchasing a card rechargeable called "Ten+", that allows you to save on travel if you have plan to use public transportation frequently. It is advisable to bring single coins to pay the exact amount of the ticket.

  • Points of Sale: In addition to paying the driver, they can also purchase tickets at the ticket offices of the main bus and bus stations tram or in some authorized shops and kiosks. Remember that it is necessary to have the ticket before boarding the bus.

  • Interconnections: The main bus stations, such as the "Estación de Santa Cruz de Tenerife" and the "Costa Adeje" Station, offer connections between different routes. This facilitates movement between different points of the island and allows to explore different areas smoothly.

Remember to plan your bus trips in advance, take into account the schedules and make sure you have the necessary ticket before boarding.

Gastronomy

Tenerife has exquisite gastronomy. Under no circumstances can they leave here without visiting a "guachinche". These are family establishments where they serve homemade dishes accompanied by local wine. Some of the most typical dishes they are the canarian ranch, ribs with potatoes or if they are more fish lovers they can try the canarian "sancocho" or the grilled octopus among many others. Visit the Teide National Park

You can't stop visiting this majestic park, home to the highest peak in Spain. Take the cable car for stunning views or venture to walk along the trails that surround the volcano. The beauty nature of this place will leave you breathless.

Relax on the beaches

Tenerife has beautiful beaches, volcanic sand and crystal clear waters. Some of the most popular are "Playa de las Teresitas", in Santa Cruz de Tenerife, and "Playa del Duque", in Costa Adeje. Enjoy the sun, give yourself a dip in the Atlantic Ocean and relax in this beach paradise. Careful with the water that is freezing!

Hiking in Anaga

If you are lovers of nature and adventure, you cannot stop exploring the Anaga Rural Park. Located in the extreme northeast of Tenerife, it offers a great variety of trails and spectacular landscapes. They will be able to walk between lush laurel forests, discover viewpoints with panoramic views to the ocean and into deep ravines. Remember to wear adequate shoes, water and enjoy the natural beauty of Anaga. Always have at hand the information you need to explore, enjoy and fall in love with the natural spaces of Tenerife. Nightlife in Tenerife

For those lovers of the night and parties, more or less moved, there are some options very close to the event.

"El Cuadrilátero", very close to a tram stop, it is the epicenter of the college nightlife on the island. In this area you will find a great number of bars, clubs and pubs, where you can enjoy live music, drink cocktails and dance until the wee hours of the morning.

If you are looking for a lively nightlife experience in Santa Cruz de Tenerife, you can't stop visiting Avenue Anaga with a wide variety of bars, pubs and discos that adapt to all tastes and styles. Here you can enjoy a festive atmosphere, live music, DJs and a great selection of drinks to delight your palate. Anaga Avenue is a meeting point popular with both residents and visitors, and is the place perfect for dancing, socializing and enjoying a fun-filled night in Santa Cruz de Tenerife.

There are many more options, further or closer, but remember not to go too far for be on top during the event!
